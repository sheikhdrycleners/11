About Us

SHEIKH DRYCLENERS was established in 1990 with the objective of providing exceptional washing, dry cleaning, stain removal, and ironing services. Our customers trust us for our quality work and timely service. Located in Kalanala, Bhavnagar, Gujarat, we have built a strong reputation in the community.

Our mission is to provide high-quality services at affordable prices, using advanced techniques and equipment to ensure customer satisfaction.

Hotel Laundry Work
